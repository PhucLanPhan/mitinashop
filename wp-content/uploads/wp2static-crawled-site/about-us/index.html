Our Team

Phuc Lan Phan

CHIEF CREATIVE OFFICER

As MITINA’s Chief Creative Officer, Phuc Lan Phan oversees the company’s award-winning creative staff, whose work encompasses all brand creative, web design, and product design efforts. Fast Company called him one of the Most Creative People in Business in 2025. Phuc has overseen five Super Bowl campaigns since joining the MITINA media staff. Prior to joining MITINA, Phuc worked as TBWA Worldwide’s Worldwide Digital Executive Creative Director and as a Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy and AKQA. He earned a master’s degree in programming and graphic design from James Cook University.

Paul Gubbay

CHIEF PRODUCT OFFICER

As the Chief Product Officer of MITINA, Paul oversees the product organization, which encompasses Product Management and Product Design. Prior to joining MITINA, Paul spent over 10 years at Adobe as VP of Design and Web, responsible for a wide span of Adobe’s Creative Cloud products including Illustrator, Indesign, XD and Spark. He joined Adobe through its acquisition of Macromedia in 2005, where he ran Product Development for Dreamweaver and Flash Professional. He developed his passion for the Web as a Co-Founder and CEO of CyberSage, a flash application software company that was acquired by Macromedia in 2003.

Anthony Casalena

FOUNDER & CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Anthony Casalena is the Founder and CEO of MITINA, which he started from his dorm room in 2003. During the company’s early years, Anthony acted as the sole engineer, designer, and support representative for the entire business. In addition to running the company and setting overall product strategy, he remains actively involved in many departments of the company that he had previously run himself. Anthony holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Maryland.
